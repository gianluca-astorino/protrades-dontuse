About Us

ProTrades OnDemand is a company based out of the Greater Toronto Area, serving the people of Canada and the USA (at the moment). Our Service provides the customers (you) with an easy, convenient, and fast way to find, locate and narrow down the best tradesworkers for your job. From small business home painters, to Industrial workers and landscapers, we provide you with a wide variety of choice from the best of the best, all through our easy to navigate website. Enter Your Location and find filtered results on workers availble to work on your project, in your area. ITS THAT SIMPLE.

Why should businesses sign up with us? Well that's easy. Businesses of any size can sign up, pay a small monthly fee and post there listings, and attract those looking for a job to be done. Gain more attraction and use us as an easy way for marketing and publicity. It's a no brainer. (for more info contact us at the support page, or to sign up as a business click here)

This solution saves you time, energy and makes your life a bunch easier. No need to waste your time calling around and getting referrals, when you can find affordable and high quality producing workers at your fingertips. We saw a hole in the market that has been happening for as long as we can remember, and we are going to change the way things operate, to make it easier and faster for you to get your needs met, beyond expectation.
